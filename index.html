The work that quietly eats your week

Most growing businesses lose hours every week to small manual tasks that never seem to stop. Updating spreadsheets, chasing information, copying data between tools, checking documents, sending the same messages, preparing reports, and trying to keep everyone aligned.

FAQ

Common questions

What is AI automation?

AI automation means using AI and software to do the repetitive, manual work your team does by hand, like sorting information, moving data between tools, drafting routine messages, and chasing updates. The result is less busywork, fewer mistakes, and more time for the work that needs a person.

What does it cost?

Every project is different, so I quote per project after the discovery call, once I understand the work involved. No retainers you don't need, no surprises.

Who is this for?

Any growing business or professional firm with repetitive manual admin: legal, accountancy, property, logistics, and other service businesses. If your team spends hours on admin that feels like it should be automated, it probably can be.

Do you only work with London businesses?

I'm based in London and work closely with London firms, but most of the work is done remotely, so I help businesses across the UK too.

How long does it take?

Small automations can be live in days. A full internal system for a whole team takes longer, but you'll see working pieces early instead of waiting months for one big reveal.

Do I need to be technical?

No. That's the whole point. I handle the technical side and keep you in the loop in plain English.

I'm not sure what to automate. Can you help?

Yes. That's what the discovery call is for. I'll help you spot the biggest time-wasters, even if you don't know where to start.
