AI automation for London businesses

I am an AI automation consultant in London. I take the repetitive admin off your team using AI and custom software, and I build the tools to do it. Here is what I do, who I help, and why it is worth a conversation.

By Rashid, founder of Layerform System Ltd, based in London.

What I do

Most growing businesses lose hours every week to manual admin: chasing documents, copying data between tools, updating spreadsheets, preparing the same reports, keeping everyone aligned. I build AI automation and custom software that takes that work off your team, so they can do more without the business grinding to a halt on paperwork.

  • Automate the repetitive admin that eats your team's time.
  • Connect the tools you already use, so the same data is never entered twice.
  • Put AI to work on real tasks: reading documents, sorting enquiries, drafting routine replies.
  • Build custom internal software when off-the-shelf products do not fit how you work.

Who it is for

I work with professional firms and local businesses across London and the UK: legal, accountancy, property and estate agents, logistics and delivery, and other service businesses. If your team spends hours each week on admin that feels like it should be automated, it usually can be.

Why work with someone who actually builds

Plenty of people will talk to you about AI. I build and run real software. That matters, because the gap between a nice slide deck and a working system is where most projects quietly fail. I have shipped software that real businesses use, so you are working with someone who has actually done it, not just advised on it.

Software I have already built and run
  • DSPOps, an operations platform for Amazon delivery partners, with an AI check that automatically detects whether a van has any damage. Live.
  • Estate Revenue Manager, a staff rota and revenue management system for high-end residential property firms.
  • A case management system for a UK immigration law firm, replacing paper files and shared inboxes with one place to work. In build.

How I work

  1. Find the manual work. A short call to spot the tasks wasting the most time.
  2. Map the workflow. We map how the work really flows, so the system fits reality.
  3. Build it. I build the AI automation and internal tools around that workflow.
  4. Connect and launch. I join it up to the tools you already use, and your team gets one clear place to work.

In London, and across the UK
